The Dogs

Charlee

Charlee is a 9 year old shepard lab mix. She loves being lazy on the couch and watching anything on the television. Despite her lazy nature, she loves to play with her little sister, Acee! Her favourite toys are anything soft and plushie. She also enjoys stealing dirty clothing out of hampers to cuddle with.

Acee

Acee is Charlee’s younger sister! Acee is a rescue dog, so her breed is unknown. She is believed to be a bully breed mix. Acee is energetic, funny and rambunxious and doesn’t let her bad past get her down! She loves food and will eat anything she can get a hold of, but her favourite food is apples!
